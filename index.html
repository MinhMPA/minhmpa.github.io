A summary of my education and research can be found in my CV. Below and in other tabs, you will find elaborate information that are hopefully useful.

I model and combine different probes of large-scale structure in our Universe to gain insights into gravity, Dark Energy, and structure formation.

I am currently a Kavli IPMU Fellow at Kavli Institute for Physics and Mathematics of the Universe, University of Tokyo. Before joining Kavli IPMU, I was a Leinwber Fellow at Leinweber Institute for Theoretical Physics, University of Michigan. Even before that, I obtained my PhD at Max Planck Institute for Astrophysics, Garching, Germany.
